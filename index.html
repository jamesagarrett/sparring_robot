Our Mission

SAMAR Robotics is an early-stage robotics startup developing an autonomous, interactive training robot designed to simulate real-world martial arts sparring. Our prototype integrates omnidirectional mobility, LIDAR-based perception, and onboard computation to dynamically reposition itself relative to a human user, maintaining safe and realistic engagement distances in real time. Our mission is to make high-quality, responsive sparring training accessible to individuals who lack consistent access to human training partners, while laying the groundwork for broader applications in interactive robotics, human–robot interaction, and embodied autonomy.

Core Features In Development

Adaptive Sparring AI

Continuously adjusts speed, power, and tactics based on your performance. Learns your fighting style and predicts your movements.

Human‑Safe Actuation

Senses objects and movement and dodges strikes for realistic but safe training.

Strike Analytics

Tracks accuracy, force, reaction time, combos, and fatigue in real time.

Martial Arts Trained

Select your sport (Taekwondo, Boxing, Muay Thai, and more) and SAMAR will adjust its fighting mode to best help you train.

Modular Armor

Swappable bags to simulate different opponents, heights, and sizes.

What's Next?

We are actively developing the next-generation mechanical platform for the SAMAR robot, transitioning from a proof-of-concept wooden prototype to a manufacturable, modular, and lightweight chassis. This includes: A redesigned structural frame (moving toward aluminum or composite materials) Optimized mounting for sensors, motors, and electronics Improved load-bearing support for freestanding training bags Modular assemblies to support future variants and accessories Tight mechanical tolerances to reduce drift and improve sensor alignment CAD is central to this work, as mechanical geometry directly affects autonomy performance, sensor visibility, stability, and safety.
